\question{Convert the following equation from polar to Cartesian coordinates.

\[r\sin\left(\theta\right)=-2.\]}{calculus/228-cartesian-coordinates-conversion.html}{13 May 2005}

\paragraph{Solution.}\partSolutionAuthor{zee}

To convert from polar coordinates to Cartesian coordinates, use the following formulas

\begin{eqnarray*}
	x&=&r\cos\left(\theta\right)\\
	y&=&r\sin\left(\theta\right).
\end{eqnarray*}

Thus, the same equation in the Cartesian coordinate system is $y=-2$.
