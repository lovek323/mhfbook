\question{A square wave has a period of 0.4 seconds. What is the frequency in hertz (Hz) of the fundamental component of the associated Fourier Series?}{calculus/248-maths-problems.html}{17 May 2005}

\index{Fourier Series}
\index{Series, Fourier}

\paragraph{Solution.} The fundamental frequency is the same as that of the square wave, that is

\[f_0=\frac{1}{\tau}=\frac{1}{0.4}=2.5\mbox{ Hz}.\]
